mission

Provide financial support to women aspiring to race bicycles at the professional level.

manifesto

organizational goals

Gather and distribute the funds, equipment and community necessary to support the racing ambitions of one woman for one year

  • Gather and distribute the funds, equipment and community necessary to support the racing ambitions of one woman for one year.
  • Facilitate the move of the sponsored woman athlete from amatuer to professional bike racing.
  • Continually grow the program to allow for an increase in the number of annually sponsored athletes.
